Petersburg Haven

Location: St. Petersburg
Rooms: 5 bedrooms
Area: 180 m²

About This Project

The Petersburg Haven is a comfortable family home designed with modern convenience and classic aesthetic touches in mind. Located in the beautiful suburbs of St. Petersburg, it offers a serene, warm atmosphere for a large family.

With an intelligently designed layout that maximizes the 180 sq m floor area, this 5-bedroom home balances shared family spaces with private retreats. The heart of the home is a spacious open-concept kitchen and living area, perfect for gatherings.

Equipped with smart home features and energy-efficient heating systems to withstand the cooler climates, the Petersburg Haven represents the zenith of cozy, reliable home construction.
