Turnkey Custom Homes

Our core expertise lies in building robust, modern homes from the ground up. We specialize in using aerated concrete blocks, renowned for their incredible thermal insulation, fire resistance, and long-term durability.

When we say "turnkey," we mean it. We manage the heavy machinery, civil engineering, structural framing, and all necessary municipal inspections.
