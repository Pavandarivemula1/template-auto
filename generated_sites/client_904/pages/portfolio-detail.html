Modern Vista Villa

Complete interior redesign featuring open-plan living and minimalist finishes tailored for maximum natural light immersion.

Project Info

Client The Evergreen Family
Location Seattle, WA
Services Interior Detailing, Furniture Selection
Completed March 2026

The Challenge

The clients approached us with a uniquely divided 1970s split-level home. They wanted to radically expand the visual flow of the space and incorporate structural glass walls without compromising the fundamental warmth of an intimate family sanctuary.

Our Solution

By deploying extensive structural reinforcements, we were able to safely eliminate the core partition walls. We utilized a completely neutral color palette augmented by heavy organic textures—such as raw oak wood and natural slate stone—to ground the newly soaring open-concept layout.
