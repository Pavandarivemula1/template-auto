Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Collect

At Real Estate Construction, we only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so—for example, to provide our architectural or construction services, to communicate with you, or to make our services better.

  • Information You Provide: Your name, email address, phone number, and project details provided via our contact form.
  • Information Provided Automatically: Basic log information such as your IP address, browser type, and date/time of access.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to communicate with you about your housing project, to provide customer support, and to improve our website and service offerings.

3. Security

While no online service is 100% secure, we work very hard to protect information about you against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction, and take reasonable measures to do so.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@realestate.com or via our contact page.
