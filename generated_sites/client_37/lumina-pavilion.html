Lumina Pavilion

Interior Architecture | Completed 2023

Concept & Philosophy

The Lumina Pavilion is an exercise in adaptive reuse. We were tasked with transforming an abandoned, early 20th-century industrial warehouse into a vibrant, modern creative studio. The goal was to preserve the historical integrity of the structure while injecting it with entirely new light and life.

We sandblasted decades of paint to reveal the original, beautifully aged masonry and exposed steel trusses. Rather than dividing the massive floor plan with traditional walls, we inserted freestanding, sound-proof glass "pavilions" throughout the space to serve as meeting rooms and private offices.

By vastly expanding the original industrial skylights, the entire interior is now flooded with diffused natural daylight. The resulting space honors its rugged, utilitarian past while providing a highly refined, collaborative environment for modern enterprise.
